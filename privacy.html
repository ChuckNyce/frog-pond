// privacy policy

what we collect
Anonymous usage analytics via PostHog — page views, button clicks, session recordings. No personal information is collected or stored. Session recordings mask all inputs.

your input
Text and images you paste into the app are sent to our API for processing. They are NOT stored, logged, or persisted after the haiku is generated. We don't read your posts.

cookies
PostHog sets cookies for analytics purposes. No advertising cookies. No tracking across other sites.

local storage
The app stores your recent haiku history and theme preference in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

third parties
PostHog (analytics) and Anthropic (AI processing). That's it. No data is sold to third parties. Ever.

contact
Questions? Find us at frogpond.lol or on x.com/bash0frog.
